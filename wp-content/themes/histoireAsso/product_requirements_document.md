## **Project PRD: Histoire Association Website Redesign - Core Pages**

**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### **1. Project Title**
Histoire Association Website Redesign: Visual Harmonization of Core Pages

### **2. Project Goal**
To redesign key pages of the "Histoire Association" website to achieve a consistent, sophisticated, and professional "Digital Archivist" aesthetic, aligning all specified pages perfectly with the established visual language of the homepage. This ensures a cohesive brand identity and an enhanced user experience across the site.

### **3. Target Audience**
*   Existing members of "Histoire Association"
*   Prospective members interested in historical reenactment in Tournai
*   History enthusiasts and general public seeking information about events and news
*   Potential partners or collaborators

### **4. Key Stakeholders**
*   Histoire Association Leadership / Project Owner
*   Design Team
*   Development Team

### **5. Core Design Principles / Brand Guidelines**
The overarching aesthetic for this redesign phase is a refined, modern "Digital Archivist" feel. This must be strictly adhered to:
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary Background: Deep charcoal / Near Black (`#121212`)
    *   Primary Accent: Gold (`#D4AF37`)
*   **Typography:** Elegant serif fonts (consistent with homepage).
*   **Imagery:** High-quality, impactful historical photography; no kitsch medieval elements.
*   **Layout & Components:**
    *   Consistency with the homepage's TopAppBar and Footer.
    *   Utilize established visual patterns from existing homepage sections (e.g., "Dernières Découvertes," "Nos Intervenants," "Une mission contre les préjugés," "Carte de la zone d'activité").
*   **Overall Tone:** High-contrast, professional, sophisticated, minimalist, and elegant.

### **6. In-Scope Pages & Specific Requirements**

#### **6.1. Toutes les infos (News) Page**
*   **Objective:** Present news articles in a visually appealing and consistent manner.
*   **Layout:** Clean grid layout, mirroring the style and component usage of the homepage's "Dernières Découvertes" section.
*   **Content Display:** Each article should feature a high-quality historical image, headline, and brief summary.
*   **Aesthetic:** Deep charcoal background, gold accents, elegant serif fonts.
*   **Consistency:** Must include the site-wide TopAppBar and Footer.

#### **6.2. Agenda des événements (Events) Page**
*   **Objective:** Clearly display upcoming events with an elegant and functional interface.
*   **Layout:** Dark, minimalist layout. Events listed as elegant cards with thin gold borders or subtle gold accents.
*   **Content Display:** Event cards should include essential details (e.g., date, title, location).
*   **Component Reuse:** Typography and overall card style should be consistent with the homepage's "Nos Intervenants" section.
*   **Functionality:** Include a "Filter by Era" function, using visual cues consistent with the homepage's historical periods (if applicable).
*   **Aesthetic:** Adhere to the high-end, professional look.

#### **6.3. Rejoindre l'Association (Join Us / Recruitment) Page**
*   **Objective:** Encourage and facilitate new memberships through an engaging and professional interface.
*   **Layout:** Utilize a high-impact, asymmetrical layout inspired by the homepage's "Une mission contre les préjugés" section (image on one side, text/form on the other).
*   **Call-to-Action:** Prominent gold call-to-action buttons.
*   **Form:** A clean, minimalist application form integrated seamlessly into the layout.
*   **Imagery:** Large, high-impact historical photography.
*   **Aesthetic:** Deep charcoal backgrounds, gold accents.

#### **6.4. Nous contacter (Contact Us) Page**
*   **Objective:** Provide clear and accessible contact information and a functional contact form.
*   **Layout:** Split layout: one side for the contact form, the other for a map.
*   **Contact Form:** Elegant contact form with input fields subtly enhanced (e.g., gold-underlined).
*   **Map:** Integrate a map component styled consistently with the homepage's "Carte de la zone d'activité."
*   **Consistency:** Must include the site-wide "Digital Archivist" header (TopAppBar) and footer.
*   **Aesthetic:** High-contrast, professional, strictly adherent to the dark mode palette.

### **7. Non-Functional Requirements / Key Considerations**

*   **Responsive Design:** All pages must be fully responsive and optimized for various screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile). A specific review of mobile versions for these pages will be required.
*   **Accessibility (WCAG):** Ensure adherence to WCAG guidelines, particularly regarding color contrast for text and interactive elements (gold on charcoal background) to ensure readability for all users.
*   **GDPR & Legal Mentions:** Given the collection of personal data via recruitment and contact forms, a clear "Mentions Légales / Politique de Confidentialité" page or section is required. This page should also adhere to the "Digital Archivist" design system.
*   **Multilingual Support:** Clarify if the website needs to support multiple languages (e.g., French/Dutch/English) due to the location in Tournai. If so, a strategy for language switching will be needed. (Current scope assumes French only unless specified).
*   **Form Data Handling:** Define the backend process for contact and recruitment forms (e.g., email notification to `info@histoireassociation.be`, data storage in a CRM or database, etc.).

### **8. Out of Scope (for this phase)**
*   New, unrequested pages (e.g., a specific "Gallery" page, dedicated "Archaeology Reports" page, etc.) unless they emerge as a dependency from the above.
*   Major backend feature development beyond form submission handling.

### **9. Success Metrics**
*   **Visual Consistency:** All redesigned pages are indistinguishable from the homepage in terms of brand aesthetic.
*   **User Engagement:** Positive feedback on the new design, potential increase in average session duration.
*   **Conversion Rates:** Increased form submissions for "Rejoindre l'Association" and "Nous contacter" pages.
*   **Reduced Bounce Rate:** Lower bounce rates on the redesigned pages.

---

This PRD provides a comprehensive guide for the design and development teams to proceed with the redesign of these core pages, ensuring all requirements and aesthetic expectations are met.
